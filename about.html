Vedant’s journey in this thrilling industry of music began when he was just 6 years old. Living in South Delhi with his family, he was exposed to the field of music at a very young age. He learned Drumming from YouTube videos & became a professional ‘Drummer’ which sparked his interest more into music. In December 2018 he was invited to play drums at the launch of Vibrant Goa and was recognised and awarded for his performance by the honourable Minister Shri Rohan Khaunte, government of Goa.

His passion for the art of mixing various songs began at age 11 when he became a professional DJ. He was recognised as the “Youngest professionally certified DJ” in the history of “Skratch DJ Academy” which is India’s leading music academy .His skillful application of mixing different genres of music to make a smashing end-product was honed by his hard work & determination. It was not later than 4 years when he went back to Skratch DJ Academy to become a professional music producer. At the young age of just 16 years, this soon to be music prodigy became a certified music producer. He further completed various other production courses with a variety of complex software to sharpen his talent. He surpassed different genres of music, with Electro Dance Music (EDM) as his favourite. He accomplished so much at such a young age starting from making new contacts, managing public relations, learning digital marketing, mastering different music genres, performing & organising various events .His love for producing music made him one of the youngest music producers in India, to be signed by an International record label.

Vedant has proven that age is just a number and with the right motivation one can reach their goals in life. All these accomplishments were not a piece of cake. These required a lot of determination, highly personalised Interpersonal communication, many sleepless nights and being an active agent in your own success.

His passion, talent and love for the profession oozes out of his work. He is a true representation of the amazing young talent in India. This boy has embarked on an amazing journey of excellence which will get boosted with the right encouragement and support
